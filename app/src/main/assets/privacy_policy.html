2. How Data Is Stored

  • On-device: All workout data, profile information, nutrition logs, and health metrics are stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync (e.g., Strava export).
  • Health Connect: We read data from Android Health Connect (steps, sleep, heart rate) with your permission. We do not write to Health Connect.
  • Firebase: Anonymous crash reports and basic usage analytics are sent to Firebase to help us improve the app. No personally identifiable workout data is sent.

3. Third-party Services

  • Strava (optional) — If you connect your Strava account, we access your activity data via OAuth. You can disconnect at any time in Settings.
  • Google Gemini — Used for AI coaching suggestions. Only anonymised, aggregated workout metrics are sent (no GPS data, no personal info).
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics — Collects anonymous crash reports and app usage statistics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above, and only to the extent necessary to provide the app's features.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app.
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data from Profile > Delete Account. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.
  • Export: You can export workouts as GPX files or sync to Strava.
  • Revoke permissions: You can revoke any permission (location, sensors, camera) in your device's Settings at any time.

6. Data Retention

Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app. Firebase crash reports are retained for 90 days. Strava data is governed by Strava's own privacy policy.

7. Security

We use Android's encrypted storage for sensitive data. Network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. API credentials are stored securely and never included in the app binary.

8. Children's Privacy

GoSteady is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
